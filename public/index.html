ℹ️ About the service

NordicWings is a Finnish online travel agency specialising in flights from Finland to the Philippines and other popular destinations. We offer competitive prices by comparing multiple airlines and booking systems.

NordicWings is a free flight search platform. We earn a small affiliate commission from our partner airlines and booking sites when you complete a booking through our links. This costs you nothing extra — the price you see on the partner site is the same as booking directly.

Yes. NordicWings is a registered business in Finland. We operate under Finnish consumer protection law and EU passenger rights regulations.

We search flights across major airlines including Finnair, Philippine Airlines, Etihad Airways, Qatar Airways, Emirates, and many more. The airlines available depend on your route and dates.

🎫 Ticket and check-in

Your booking reference is shown on the confirmation screen immediately after payment. Please save or screenshot this reference. Contact us at hello@nordicwings.net with your booking reference and we will send your e-ticket to your email.

Check-in is done directly with the airline, not through NordicWings. Visit the airline's official website and enter your booking reference (PNR) and last name. Most airlines open online check-in 24–48 hours before departure.

Baggage allowance depends on the fare type. If you need to add extra baggage, you can do so directly on the airline's website using your booking reference after purchase. We are working on adding in-app baggage options soon.

Finnish citizens and EU residents do not need a visa for the Philippines for stays up to 30 days. You need a valid passport (at least 6 months validity beyond your travel dates) and a return or onward ticket. Always check the latest entry requirements with the Philippine embassy before travelling.

Cancellation and change policies depend on the airline and fare type. Most economy fares are non-refundable or have a change fee. Contact us at hello@nordicwings.net as soon as possible and we will help you with the options available for your booking.

💸 Cancellations & Refunds

Yes, you can request a cancellation by emailing us at hello@nordicwings.net with your booking reference number. Please note that whether a refund is possible depends on the airline's fare rules for your ticket type. Most low-cost fares are non-refundable.

Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before your scheduled departure. Requests made after this deadline cannot be processed. We recommend cancelling as early as possible to maximize your chance of a refund.

Refunds depend on the fare type purchased:

  • Flexible / refundable fares — full refund of the airline fare minus the NordicWings service fee.
  • Standard economy fares — partial refund of taxes only in most cases (airline base fare is non-refundable).
  • Promotional / sale fares — typically non-refundable, no refund issued.

The NordicWings service fee is non-refundable in all cases.

Once your cancellation is approved and the refund is processed, it typically takes 5–10 business days to appear on your original payment method. The exact timing depends on your bank or card provider.

If the airline cancels your flight, you are entitled to a full refund of the ticket price. Please email us at hello@nordicwings.net with your booking reference and we will process your refund as quickly as possible.

Date changes (rebooking) depend on the airline's policy for your fare. Contact us at hello@nordicwings.net with your booking reference and desired new dates. Change fees set by the airline may apply, plus a NordicWings rebooking fee of €25.
